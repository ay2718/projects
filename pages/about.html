About Me

I am currently a sophomore at MIT (class of 2021), majoring in electrical engineering and computer science. Although feedback systems are my primary interest, I like working on most sufficiently mathematical problems. When I have free time, I work on projects ranging in scope from room improvements to an Arduino-based quadcopter. I have tentative plans to start working on a dual resonant solid state Tesla coil once I finish my motorized shopping cart.
